Understand your glucose patterns.

Eating Glasses is a wellness app for people living with insulin-dependent diabetes who use a continuous glucose monitor. It reads your glucose patterns from Apple Health and helps you explore safe, everyday lifestyle experiments — so your numbers finally mean something to you.

Your glucose data stays on your device. Eating Glasses is built around general wellness and self-understanding, with your care team always at the center of any treatment.

Eating Glasses provides general wellness and educational information only. It is not medical advice and does not diagnose, treat, or recommend changes to your medication. Always work with your healthcare provider for treatment decisions.
